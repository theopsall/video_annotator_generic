Welcome to the Shot Annotator

The purpose of the specific web app is to randomly show shots to annotators, in order to choose the corresponding shot class.

How to use:

First you have to log in, if you have not already singed up, in order to know the annotator's profile.
Then, you have to watch a randomly selected shot and you have to provide the shot that better describes the current shot.
The more shots you annotate, the better dataset is made.
