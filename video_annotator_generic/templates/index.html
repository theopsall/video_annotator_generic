Welcome to the Video Annotator

The purpose of the specific web app is to randomly show videos to annotators, in order to select the most important timestamps over the entire video.

How to use:

First you have to log in, if you have not already singed up, in order to know the annotator's profile.
Then, you have to watch a randomly selected video and you have to annotate the most representative moments to compose a video summary.
The more video you annotate, the better dataset is made {% endblock %}
